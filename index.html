Destination

Ladakh

Ladakh is a remote and culturally distinct region in India's far north, known for its barren high-altitude desert landscape and abundance of mountains and side valleys. The region offers exciting hiking opportunities, including the Markha Valley, Hemis National Park, Zanskar Valley, and Nubra Valley treks.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a state in northern India, known for its picturesque Himalayan landscapes and trekking opportunities. The region offers several hiking trails, including the Hampta Pass Trek, the Chandratal Lake Trek, and the Pin Parvati Pass Trek.

Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a state in northern India, known for its stunning Himalayan landscapes and trekking opportunities. The region offers several hiking trails, including the Roopkund Lake Trek, the Valley of Flowers Trek, and the Kedarnath Trek.
